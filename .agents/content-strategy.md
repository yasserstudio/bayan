# Bayan — Content Strategy

*Last updated: 2026-05-20*

## Core Insight
For Bayan v1, content IS the product. The demo site is a concept — real value comes from education, awareness, and documenting the problem.

## Content Pillars

### 1. بيانات مزوّرة (Fake Announcements) — 50% of effort
Document the problem. Expose fakes. Make it undeniable.
- Fake announcement exposés
- "Real or fake?" challenges
- Ministry fake tracker (monthly)
- "How this fake was made" breakdowns
- Victim stories

### 2. وعي رقمي (Digital Security) — 30% of effort
Teach protection. Deliver value today.
- "How to spot a fake" tips
- Official ministry URLs reference
- "Before you share, check" graphics
- Phishing/scam awareness

### 3. البناء أمام الجميع (Build in Public) — 20% of effort
The tech entrepreneur story.
- Development updates
- Design decisions
- v2 progress
- Algerian tech commentary

## Platform Strategy

### Facebook ★★★★★ (primary — where Algeria lives)
- 70% fake exposés, 20% tips, 10% Bayan news
- Post in large groups (education, jobs, wilaya-specific)
- Arabic/Darija only. Images and short videos.
- Optimize for WhatsApp screenshot-sharing

### TikTok ★★★★ (discovery engine)
- "Real or fake?" challenges, quick tips, concept demos
- Darija exclusively. Visual-first.

### Twitter ★★★★ (tech community)
- Build-in-public, fake commentary, technical insights
- Threads for launch and milestones

### LinkedIn ★★★ (professional/media)
- Long-form problem analysis, personal story
- Target: journalists, professionals, gov-adjacent

### YouTube ★★ (evergreen)
- Low frequency, high quality
- Launch video: "Algeria's fake announcement problem"
- Tutorial: "How to verify any announcement"

## Content Mix
- 80% shareable (virality, awareness)
- 20% searchable (SEO foundation for v2)

## Minimum Viable Schedule
3 posts/week across platforms:
- 2x Facebook (exposé + tip)
- 1x Twitter (thread or commentary)
- Add TikTok and LinkedIn when rhythm established

## Real-Time Content Rule
When a new fake announcement circulates — drop everything and post about it. This is the highest-value content moment. React within hours, not days.

## v1 On-Site Content (delivers value now)
1. List of all official ministry websites
2. "How to spot a fake" visual guide
3. Gallery of confirmed fakes with analysis
4. Official vs. fake side-by-side comparisons
5. Digital security basics
