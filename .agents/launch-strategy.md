# Bayan — Launch Strategy

*Last updated: 2026-05-20*

## Launch Type
Civic tech public good — build-in-public. Not a SaaS launch. Success = awareness + shares, not signups or revenue.

## ORB Channels

### Owned
- bayan.yasser.studio (hub)
- Personal social accounts (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
- GitHub repo (if open-sourced)

### Rented (where Algerian citizens are)
- Facebook ★★★★★ — #1 platform in Algeria, where fakes spread
- YouTube ★★★★ — Darija explainer video potential
- TikTok ★★★★ — short-form hooks, young audience
- Telegram ★★★ — where announcements circulate
- Instagram ★★★ — carousels, reels

### Borrowed
- Algerian tech influencers/YouTubers
- Algerian news sites (TSA, Echorouk, Ennahar)
- Facebook group admins (education, jobs)
- Algerian tech communities (DZ Developers)

## Phase 1: Teasers (D-14 to D-1)
- Share real fake announcement examples
- "I'm building something to fix this" teaser
- "How to spot a fake" educational content
- Poll: "Have you been fooled?"
- Before/after mockup preview
- Demo screen recording
- "Tomorrow" announcement

## Phase 2: Launch Day
- Site goes live
- Post in 10+ large Algerian Facebook groups
- Twitter launch thread
- LinkedIn announcement
- YouTube/TikTok explainer
- DM influencers
- Pitch news sites

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (Week 1-2)
- Share reactions and metrics
- Respond to every comment
- "What I learned" reflection post
- Begin digital security tips content

## Phase 4: Sustained
- Weekly security tips
- Document new fakes as they appear
- Share v2 development progress
- Monthly "fake announcements report"

## Don't
- Product Hunt / BetaList / HN (wrong audience)
- Paid ads (no budget, organic is the strategy)
- Waitlist / exclusivity (open access from day one)
- English-first content (audience is Arabic/Darija/French)
- Political framing (never position against government)

## v1 Credibility Gap Mitigations
1. Be upfront that it's a concept
2. Make the demo polished and interactive
3. Digital security tips deliver real value now
4. Before/after document mockup is the hero visual
5. Announce v2 timeline

## Success Metrics (v1 Launch Week)
- 5,000+ site visits
- 500+ social shares
- 2-3 media mentions
- 100+ Facebook comments
- 10+ "I was fooled" user stories
